About Quandary

In The Keeper, a young girl, misunderstood and smart-shamed by her peers, becomes deeply introspective, questioning the nature of morality and existence. Her classmates ignored her unique insights, and now, they find themselves mysteriously trapped in a dreamscape set in her bedroom, a surreal world between reality and fiction. In this maze, they confront moral dilemmas, questioning good, evil, and the nature of morality—mirroring the judgments they once passed on her. Through the girl's “madness”, each must face their own flaws and the very questions they had dismissed, seeking a way to escape this haunting, introspective labyrinth.
